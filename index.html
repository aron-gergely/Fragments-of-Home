Concept:

⠀

This project serves as an exploration of the hybrid essay, a form that blends various media to present complex, fragmented narratives. By combining text and images in a non-linear, random manner, the app mimics the hybrid essay's open-ended and associative nature. Like a visual or collage essay, the project encourages users to experiment with narrative construction, revealing how seemingly unrelated elements—text and image—can coexist and interact to create new meanings, interpretations, and visual stories.

⠀

⠀

How to use:

⠀

01. — Create image-text pairs using the [new image] and [new text] buttons, or generate random pairs by clicking [randomize].

⠀

02. — Add the combinations you like to your workspace (right side) as cards with the arrow button [➔].

⠀

03. — Organize the cards to your liking: Drag them around, create new associations or stories by experimenting with the layout.

⠀

04. — Additionally, you can save your layout as an image to your device. (Coming soon...)
